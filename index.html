Frequently Asked Questions

What is DEKUTCONNECT?

DEKUTCONNECT is an independent student-run community platform for Dedan Kimathi University of Technology students, focused on campus entertainment, news, and student discussions.

How can I support DEKUTCONNECT?

You can support us by:

  • Making a donation to help with platform costs
  • Purchasing from our merchandise store
  • Sharing campus news and content with us
  • Advertising your business or services
  • Following and sharing our social media content
  • Engaging with our posts and community
and any other ways that provide value to our community. Welcome to our community!

How do I advertise with DEKUTCONNECT?

Click the "Advertise" button to fill out our quick form. Our team will get back to you within 48 hours with student-focused advertising packages tailored to your needs.

How can I contact DEKUTCONNECT?

You can contact us directly via email, WhatsApp, or Instagram DM. We also offer anonymous contact options for those who prefer privacy while sharing campus information.

What kind of content do you share?

We provide:

  • Campus entertainment and discussions
  • Student trends & campus life
  • Perspectives from DeKUT community
  • Snapshot of campus culture
  • HELB & KUCCPS updates
  • Bursary information
and other content relevant to DeKUT students.

Is DEKUTCONNECT officially affiliated with the university?

No, DEKUTCONNECT is an independent student-run platform and is not officially affiliated with Dedan Kimathi University of Technology. We operate as a community initiative by and for students.
